/ABOUT

The recognizable traits in his works probably typify characters that belong to our collective imagination of breadth as well as cosmopolitan. “Pablo” is present but not only him, that is, him with his background, his friends, lovers, confessions, impossible choices such as opposing a ruthless and unspeakable dictatorship, Pablo and his friends who have produced so much, each with their own tools and skills in the most varied fields.

Enrico's eyes observe, and it is good to observe and remember. Thus, to leave nothing to chance, the painter plays with the dualism of color and form and the front-profile to give us in this new century a double and non-monotonous vision of things and reality.

Modigliani is also on site and on tiptoe as he used to do, he bursts in thunderously, transporting the artist into an unlikely representation of a collective imagination. Again, the dualism of color and shape, front-profile to give us, in this new century, a double and non-monotonous vision of things and reality. That's good!

The trait d'union between popular-ethnic wisdom and the impulse towards the unknown is striking but always remembering that there is no variable between the "invented" present time, past and future, the brand is not in the form but in the profile of the colors where the mystery of art lies and what a painter-artist magically knows how to recognize and make us known.

/THE SICILIAN PERIOD

Enrico Gasparri was born in Italy in the city of Catania, on November 23, 1976. Having graduated in Law, he has actually always been attracted to the world of art. Thus, despite his legal studies, he embarked on an artistic journey and dedicated himself to painting, theater and music.

In this period he strengthened his relationships with the native population and discovered that he had an innate sensitivity towards Indian cultures and, more generally, towards ethnic minorities, often innocent victims of abuse by the "First World".

Here together with the master Antonio Garcia Mulet, disciple of Mirò.

/THE SPAIN PERIOD

In 2004 he moved to Valencia for 8 years.
Here he studies Dramatic Art at the ART OFF Academy and works for about a year at the Teatro del Carme. In these years he goes through a bohemian period between sleepless nights, parties, excesses... and it is precisely in this same period that he establishes a close friendship with Aurelio Delgado - historic artistic director of the Teatro del Carme - who will leave a strong mark on the artist's career.
Subsequently he came into contact with several painters, sculptors and free thinkers and only in 2012 did he move to the Balearic Islands together with his partner, Krisztina Csiki. Here, in the silence and peace of the island of Mallorca, his only and adored daughter grows up: Giulietta. These are, therefore, the years of love, inspiration and great artistic production.

Mediterranean

Subsequently, during trips to Morocco and Tunisia, he discovers and approaches the Arab culture which awakens in him memories and smells of childhood in what was the cradle of the Mediterranean: Sicily, right there where it all begins.

/THE CUBAN PERIOD

For a period he lived in Baracoa (Cuba) and his artistic production began to "fuse" the different artistic/cultural influences of African, Indian, Haitian and Cuban peoples and art. From this moment his works are tinged with vivid Caribbean colors which together with the strong ones of the Mediterranean will give life to a powerful and personal Cubist style.

WOMAN & POETRY

/THE RETURN

In 2021, separation from his partner, depression and loneliness push the artist to experiment with new techniques and break some patterns of the past. His works begin to have new elements that will lead him to a path that he himself defines as "cathartic": industrial colors dipped in oil, acrylic and glue, spatulas and hands take the place of brushes to inaugurate his new and original style.
